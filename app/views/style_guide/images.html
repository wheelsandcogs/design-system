Images and image use

Images can add visual interest to a page. They should be used with consideration and be relevant to the content. Images shouldnâ€™t be used to fill space or as decoration.

Where images are used, the photographic style should focus on people, be inclusive and reflect diversity. Do not use clip art, staged or cliched stock photography. Do not place text on top of an image, use text as an image, or add unnecessary filters or effects.

All images must be free from usage restrictions and permissible for use. Please contact the central design team for advice.
