Images and image use

Images can add visual interest to a page. They should be used with consideration and be relevant to the content. Images must not be used to fill space or as decoration.

Where images are used, the photographic style should focus on people, be inclusive and reflect the diversity of the people we serve. Do not use clip art or cliched stock photography. Make images accessible. Do not place text on top of an image, use text as an image, or add unnecessary filters or effects.

All images must be free from usage restrictions and permissible for use.

Please contact the central design team (design@digital.homeoffice.gov.uk) for advice.
